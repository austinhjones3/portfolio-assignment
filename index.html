Ordinary Guy, Extraordinary Passion

Hello there, I'm Austin Jones. I'm from New Orleans, LA, and I have a long history of customer service which I have translated to web development career.

About Austin

When I was a teenager, I had many ideas for blogs, websites, and web marketing. I would spend a decent amount of my free-time trying to piece together these ideas without any technical knowledge. It is only natural that I grew up to learn how to make these ideas a reality.

I have found that my best work comes out when I work with other engineers. Teaching others helps me learn faster and learn topics deeper than I would have alone. Likewise, someone pointing out my mistakes helps me to understand why it is a mistake in the first place.

In my free time, I enjoy working out, reading non-fiction, watching dramas and animes, listening to music, hanging out with my fianc√© and daughter, and working on side projects.

I can build fully functional inanimate webpages, back-end node.js services, as well as help you receive and use information from other websites.
